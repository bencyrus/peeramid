Peeramid is a collaboration platform

We aim to ease up the process of entering a profession as easy as possible for applicants, and hiring talent as easy as possible for recruiters and companies

Peeramid allows users to create projects and seek partners to work on them, find projects that already exist and collaborate on them.

Users showcase their hard and soft skills and level of expertise through projects they work on and publish, as well as building networks based on real professional collaborations

recruiters also can browse open-to-work users and judge them based on the projects they have worked on and hands on abilities they have rather than relying on a certificate or proof of qualification

after all, "Good wine needs no bush!"
